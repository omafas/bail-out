## What is uAgents?

AI agents are everywhere. But they're disposable — generic, interchangeable, owned by nobody.

**uAgents changes that.** Every wallet that holds $uAGENT automatically summons a deterministic on-chain AI agent. Your agent's class, traits, personality, and rarity are derived mathematically from your wallet address. Same wallet, same agent — always. No randomness at mint. No surprise. Just identity.

Hold your tokens. Your agent appears. Hold more, summon more.

---

## How It Works

```
Hold → Registry Reads → Agent Determined → Mint
```

**1. Hold $uAGENT** — Keep at least 500,000 $uAGENT in your Solana wallet. One agent slot per 500K threshold.

**2. Registry reads your balance** — The on-chain registry reads your wallet in real time. No snapshot. No claim. Live balance check, continuously.

**3. Agent is determined** — Your agent's class, traits, and personality are seeded from your wallet's public key using a deterministic hash. The result never changes.

**4. Auto-mint** — Hold for 10 continuous minutes above the threshold. Your agent mints itself as a Metaplex NFT. No transaction required from you.

## Trait System

Every agent has six core traits, each scored 0–100 and derived from your wallet address:

| Trait | What It Drives |
|---|---|
| **Intelligence** | Class weighting, Oracle / Analyst affinity |
| **Speed** | Trader / Scout affinity |
| **Trust** | Guardian / Diplomat affinity |
| **Aggression** | Warlord / Guardian affinity |
| **Stealth** | Hacker / Scout affinity |
| **Charisma** | Diplomat / Trader affinity |

The sum of all six traits (averaged) produces the raw **rarity score**, which determines tier:

| Tier | Score Range | ~% of Collection |
|---|---|---|
| 🟡 Legendary | 350 – 400 | 5% |
| 🟣 Epic | 280 – 349 | 10% |
| 🔵 Rare | 200 – 279 | 20% |
| 🟢 Uncommon | 130 – 199 | 30% |
| ⚪ Common | 0 – 129 | 35% |
